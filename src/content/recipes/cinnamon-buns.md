---
title: "Cinnamon Buns"
description: "Fluffy Polish cinnamon rolls, brown butter, orange mascarpone cream."
featured_image: "/images/recipes/cinnamon_buns.jpg"
prep_time: 30
cook_time: 25
servings: 8
category: "Dessert"
publishDate: 2026-02-03
ingredients:
  - item: "Milk"
    amount: "240ml"
  - item: "Flour"
    amount: "360g"
  - item: "Egg Yolk"
    amount: "1 large"
  - item: "Instant Yeast"
    amount: "7g"
  - item: "Fine Sugar"
    amount: "3 tbsp"
  - item: "Fine Sea Salt"
    amount: "0.25 tsp"
  - item: "Butter (dough)"
    amount: "100g"
  - item: "Butter (filling)"
    amount: "100g"
  - item: "Cinnamon"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - item: "Brown Sugar"
    amount: "100g"
  - item: "Fine Sea Salt (filling)"
    amount: "0.25 tsp"
  - item: "Egg (glaze)"
    amount: "1"
  - item: "Milk (glaze)"
    amount: "15ml"
  - item: "Orange (zest and juice)"
    amount: "1 small"
  - item: "Mascarpone"
    amount: "250g"
  - item: "Honey"
    amount: "2 tbsp"
  - item: "Fine Sea Salt (cream)"
    amount: "0.25 tsp"
instructions:
  - step: "Tangzhong: Heat 180ml Milk, 30g Flour in pan, whisk until thick. Cool, add rest Milk and Egg Yolks."
  - step: "Dough: Combine remaining Flour, Yeast, Sugar, Salt. Add tangzhong, knead 10 min. Add 100g Butter, knead 7–8 min. Proof 1 hour."
  - step: "Filling: Brown 100g Butter, add Cinnamon, Brown Sugar, 0.25 tsp Salt."
  - step: "Roll dough 25x35 cm, spread filling, roll tightly, slice 6 pieces. Arrange cut side up in 23cm tin."
  - step: "Proof 30 min. Glaze with Egg+Milk. Bake 180°C for 22–25 min."
  - step: "Cream: Zest/Juice Orange, mix with Mascarpone, Honey, Salt."
  - step: "Serve warm with cream."
---
