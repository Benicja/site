---
title: "Shortbread"
description: "Classic buttery shortbread, soft and crumbly."
featured_image: "/images/recipes/shortbread.jpeg"
prep_time: 10
cook_time: 30
servings: 18
category: "Dessert"
publishDate: 2026-01-31
ingredients:
  - item: "Unsalted Butter, Cubed"
    amount: "200g"
  - item: "Caster Sugar (plus extra for finishing)"
    amount: "100g"
  - item: "Vanilla Extract (optional)"
    amount: "1 tsp"
  - item: "Plain Flour (plus extra for dusting)"
    amount: "300g"
instructions:
  - step: "Preheat oven to 170°C."
  - step: "Mix Butter, Sugar, (Vanilla). Stir in Flour, form dough."
  - step: "Press into tin, prick, sprinkle Sugar."
  - step: "Bake 25–35 min."
---
