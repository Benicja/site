## üçõ Tarka Dal

**Category:** Dinner  
**Prep Time:** 10 min  
**Cook Time:** 35 min  
**Servings:** 4

**Description:**  
A comforting Indian lentil dish, perfect for a delicious vegetarian meal or with chicken for added protein. Serve with rice for a complete meal.

**Ingredients:**
- 0.5 Large Onion
- 4 Garlic Cloves
- 1 Head Broccoli
- 1000g Chicken Breasts
- 200g Red Split Lentils
- 1 Tin Chickpeas (substitute for Chicken)
- 200g Basmati Rice
- Olive Oil
- 1 Tablespoon Cumin
- 1 Teaspoon Turmeric
- 1 Teaspoon Chili Powder
- 3 Teaspoon Smoked Paprika
- Salt
- Pepper

**Methodology:**
1. Prepare a wok and saucepan.  
2. Put Basmati Rice into frying pan and cover with cold water. Leave to soak.
3. Steam cook Broccoli in saucepan.
4. Dice Onion, Garlic and Chicken.
5. After 8 minutes, drain Broccoli, then transfer soaked Rice into saucepan and boil. Top up with more hot water until Rice is fluffy.
6. Add chopped Onion to frying pan and fry in Olive Oil for 5 minutes until golden, then add Garlic, Cumin, and Chili Powder. Fry everything together for 5 more minutes.
7. Remove Onion-Garlic-Spice mixture ("tarka"). Set aside. Add diced Chicken to frying pan with more Olive Oil. Fry 5 minutes until browned, then add Smoked Paprika, Salt, Pepper, and fry 5 more minutes.
8. When Rice is fluffy and water fully absorbed, set aside.
9. Rinse Lentils and add to saucepan. Cover with water and bring to a boil.
10. Add Turmeric to Lentils, then add cooked Chicken or drained Chickpeas. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
11. When Lentils soften, mix in tarka.
12. Serve with Rice and steamed Broccoli.

## ü•ß Leek And Chicken Pie

**Category:** Dinner  
**Prep Time:** 15 min  
**Cook Time:** 25 min  
**Servings:** 4‚Äì6

**Description:**  
A hearty Joe Wicks-inspired chicken pie, creamy and satisfying with tender chicken, leeks, sweet potato, and flaky pastry.

**Ingredients:**
- 2 Leeks
- 3 Sweet Potatoes
- 800g Chicken Breast
- 100g Unsalted Butter
- 200ml Double Cream or Milk
- 1 Large Pack Puff Pastry (or 500g Pasta)
- 15g Chicken Stock (2-3 Cubes)
- Smoked Paprika
- 200g Rice

**Methodology:**
1. Prepare a wok and saucepan.
2. Preheat oven to 200¬∞C (180¬∞C fan).
3. Dice Leeks, Sweet Potato and Chicken.
4. Fry Leeks and Chicken in wok with Butter until browned.
5. Fry Sweet Potato in saucepan with Oil for 5 minutes, then coat in Smoked Paprika and fry for 5 more minutes. Add to wok.
6. Add Rice to saucepan, mix briefly, soften in a little boiling water for 5 minutes. Add to wok.
7. Mix Chicken Stock with boiling water, pour into wok, simmer for 5 minutes.
8. Stir in Double Cream, simmer until thickened.
9. Pour mixture into baking dish. Cover with Puff Pastry, decorate.
10. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden and crisp.
11. Serve hot, optionally with Broccoli.

**Prep Time:** 5 min  
**Cook Time:** 25 min  
**Servings:** 4

## Cinnamon Buns

**Category:** Breakfast  
**Prep Time:** 30 min (+1‚Äì1.5 hour rise)  
**Cook Time:** 25 min  
**Servings:** 6‚Äì8

**Description:**  
Fluffy Polish cinnamon rolls, brown butter, orange mascarpone cream.

**Ingredients:**  
- 240ml Milk
- 360g Flour
- 1 Large Egg Yolk
- 7g Instant Yeast
- 3 Tablespoon Fine Sugar
- 0.25 Teaspoon Fine Sea Salt
- 100g Butter (dough)
- 100g Butter (filling)
- 2 Tablespoon Cinnamon
- 100g Brown Sugar
- 0.25 Teaspoon Fine Sea Salt (filling)
- 1 Egg (glaze)
- 15ml Milk (glaze)
- 1 Small Orange (zest and juice)
- 250g Mascarpone
- 2 Tablespoon Honey
- 0.25 Teaspoon Fine Sea Salt (cream)

**Methodology:**
1. Tangzhong: Heat 180ml Milk, 30g Flour in pan, whisk until thick. Cool, add rest Milk and Egg Yolks.
2. Dough: Combine remaining Flour, Yeast, Sugar, Salt. Add tangzhong, knead 10 min. Add 100g Butter, knead 7‚Äì8 min. Proof 1 hour.
3. Filling: Brown 100g Butter, add Cinnamon, Brown Sugar, 0.25 tsp Salt.
4. Roll dough 25x35 cm, spread filling, roll tightly, slice 6 pieces. Arrange cut side up in 23cm tin.
5. Proof 30 min. Glaze with Egg+Milk. Bake 180¬∞C for 22‚Äì25 min.
6. Cream: Zest/Juice Orange, mix with Mascarpone, Honey, Salt.
7. Serve warm with cream.

## Gosia's Meringue Cake
